Mission

IEEE’s core purpose is to foster technological innovation and excellence for the benefit of humanity. The members commit themselves to the highest of integrity, responsible behavior and professional conduct.

Vision

IEEE’s core purpose is to foster technological innovation and excellence for the benefit of humanity. The members commit themselves to the highest of integrity, responsible behavior and professional conduct. To be essential to the global technical community and to technical professionals everywhere, and be universally recognized for the contributions of technology and of technical professionals in improving global conditions.
